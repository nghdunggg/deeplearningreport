Dataset Overview

The DBpedia14 dataset is a collection of Wikipedia entities categorized into 14 distinct classes.

  • Training Samples: 560,000
  • Test Samples: 70,000
  • Features: label, title, content

Class Distribution

The dataset is perfectly balanced, with exactly 40,000 training samples per class. The 14 classes include:

Company, EducationalInstitution, Artist, Athlete, OfficeHolder, MeanOfTransportation, Building, NaturalPlace, Village, Animal, Plant, Album, Film, WrittenWork.
