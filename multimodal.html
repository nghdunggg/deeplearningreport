Methodology

Our approach utilizes the ViT-B/32 CLIP backbone to extract features from both images and their corresponding text descriptions. We tested three distinct scenarios:

  1. Zero-Shot: Direct prediction without any training.
  2. Few-Shot (k=5): Training with only 5 samples per class (505 total samples).
  3. Full Training: Fine-tuning on the entire dataset (67,988 samples).

Performance Comparison

The following table summarizes the test accuracy achieved across the three scenarios:

Scenario Sample Size Epochs Accuracy
Zero-Shot 0 - 59.08%
Few-Shot (k=5) 505 30 82.85%
Full Training 67,988 20 91.02%

Training Configuration

  • Optimizer: Adam
  • Learning Rate: 1e-3
  • Weight Decay: 1e-4 (L2 Regularization)
  • Batch Size: 256 for full train / 32 for few-shot
